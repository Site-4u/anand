About Structech

STRUCTECH CONSULTING ENGINEERS (INDIA) PVT LTD. is dedicated to providing the best solution & results critical to the mutual productivity of our client & organization, through the use of our quality design, consulting services, & computer programs for any architectural & engineering job. STRUCTECH's quest is to be an organization of knowledge workers using information technology to socially reconstruct & transfer knowledge & knowledge's by creating new perspectives providing competitive advantage to its customers. STRUCTECH provides innovative, cost-effective solution to a wide range of consulting , research & design services in the areas of Architectural, Structural, Civil, Evaluation & Retrofitting of existing Structures, Electrical, HVAC, Plumbing, Fire Fighting & Computer Aided Engineering.

As services are available under one roof objectives of quality, value, time, schedule & costs are achieved

  • Well qualified and internationally experienced engineers and designers.
  • International standards and quality control systems
  • Technical personnel in all disciplines under one roof
  • In house computers, printers, plotters, blue printing, photo copy, jumbo photo-copying and other infrastructure facilities
  • Partnering with client for on time project completion and within budget, by strict monitoring.
  • Management reliance on TQM (Total Quality Management) to ensure superior co-ordination at various levels.
