Evaluation & Retrofitting of Existing Structures

STRUCTECH multi disciplinary teams provide a comprehensive approach to Repair & Rehabilitation Projects, from investigation through construction observation. Knowledge gained through applied research & various prior investigations enables STRUCTECH to develop practical, innovative solutions to problems of existing buildings & structures. STRUCTECH has provided investigation, design and construction observation services for a wide variety of repairs & rehabilitation projects. STRUCTECH develops repairs with appropriate materials & state-of-art procedures, evaluated with in-house laboratory testing & field expertise. Experience gained from many of past investigations, coupled with comprehensive testing and capabilities make STRUCTECH a leader in the assessment of existing structures. STRUCTECH project teams use a wide variety of non-destructive diagnostic techniques to asses the condition of existing building, bridges, and other specialised structures. Special capabilities include in-place performance testing, field & laboratory evaluation & advanced computer modeling. Services regularly provided include façade & roof evaluations, structural analysis, condition surveys and pre-purchase inspections. Special services include seismic resistance studies, load tests, capital reserve studies and maintenance program to extend the serviceable life of buildings & structures.
